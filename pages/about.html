About Preedos Kenya

Your trusted partner in vertical transportation solutions

Our Story

Building trust through excellence since our inception

Founded in 2010, Preedos Kenya Limited has grown to become one of the leading providers of elevator and escalator solutions in Kenya. What started as a small team of passionate engineers has evolved into a full-service company known for reliability, innovation, and exceptional customer service.

Our journey has been marked by numerous successful projects across various sectors, including commercial, residential, hospitality, and industrial. We take pride in our ability to deliver customized solutions that meet the unique needs of each client while maintaining the highest standards of safety and quality.

Our Mission

To provide innovative, reliable, and safe vertical transportation solutions that enhance mobility and accessibility while exceeding customer expectations through exceptional service and technical expertise.

Our Vision

To be the leading provider of elevator and escalator solutions in East Africa, recognized for innovation, safety, and customer satisfaction.
